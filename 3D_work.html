HIMAWARI & ASTROMIAUTA
Himawari and Astromiauta are two original characters I created and animated, drawing inspiration from the popular Spanish songs "Himawari" by Axolotes Mexicanos and "En qué estrella estará" by Nena Daconte. Their design reflects my admiration for early 2000s video game aesthetics and Japanese pop culture.

LORD BARTHOLOMEW MONTAGUE
This character was designed and modeled with the concept of being an elderly boxer. My proposal focused on a former aristocrat ruined by his own greed, who turned to street boxing as a last resort. However, his lack of skill left him in an even worse state, embodying both his downfall and his struggle to survive. It is fully hand-painted and sculpted.
